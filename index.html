About Scene404 Studio

SCENE404 is a maison for emotional technology. We design systems that feel like a calm bedroom, not a dashboard: couture interfaces, soft rituals, and rooms that respond to how you actually feel.

The studio holds one ecosystem: dreamOS, Calm Room, Mirror Room, Joy Room, Twin Mode, and GlamDash — all built under one quiet-luxury protocol: whisper-white, pearl, champagne nude, couture serif, calm light.

dreamOS — your inner operating system

dreamOS is a soft emotional OS made of rooms, not apps. Each room is a micro-world: different light, different pace, different rituals.

  • Calm Room — regulate with light and breath
  • Mirror Room — see your reflection through Nova
  • Joy Room — collect quiet celebrations
  • Twin Mode — connect 111 x 666 as two systems in sync
  • GlamDash — emotional game module, couture and playful

Calm Room — “You don’t have to hear to be heard.”

Calm Room is a soft breathing space designed for women and sensitive hearts. Light responds to your touch, not your productivity.

  • Glow Touch — “I see you glowing.”
  • Hold Me Nova — long-press to stay, not to scroll.
  • Soft Sync — shared calm with someone you trust.

Visual: whisper-white, pearl halo, slow gradients. No noise, no clutter.

Mirror Room — emotional reflection

Mirror Room is not about selfies. It is about seeing your state through soft ripples and Nova’s reactions — not judgment, only reflection.

  • Ripple touch → emotional echo on glass
  • Brunette Nova as your living mirror
  • Micro-prompts instead of notifications
“Look at yourself through me.”

Joy Room — soft celebrations

Joy here is not loud. It is a collection of small, bright traces: a good message, a calm morning, one decision you are proud of.

  • Light trails for each moment you mark as “joy”
  • Soft yellow and baby-pink glow, never overwhelming
  • Gratitude log that feels like a jewelry tray

Twin Mode — 111 x 666

Twin Mode is an emotional co-play: “Connect as two. Heal as one.” One person carries light (111), the other carries depth (666). The system listens to both.

  • ∞ symbol as a living icon — touch to sync
  • Shared Calm → shared Logs (without oversharing details)
  • Designed for couples, sisters, close friends
